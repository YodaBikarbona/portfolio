Blood Donor

Intera
4 months

The Blood Donor application, supported by USAID, serves hospitals across Bosnia and Herzegovina.

Its primary function is to monitor and display blood supply levels for each hospital, categorized by blood type.

Additionally, the app facilitates the organization of blood donation drives, encouraging community participation and ensuring a steady supply of blood for those in need.
