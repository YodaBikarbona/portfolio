ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) system is a comprehensive software solution that integrates and manages a company's core business processes, such as finance, supply chain, human resources, and manufacturing, into a unified system.

By centralizing data and automating workflows, ERP systems enhance efficiency, provide real-time insights, and support better decision-making across the organization.

Project role
Fullstack developer
Responsibility
  • Implement new ERP modules, update existing.
  • Choosing the right technologies for our solutions.
  • Modeling the data layer.
  • Generating and designing complex reports (HTML, SQL).
  • Making sure that the modules are stable and working without any problems.
Technologies & Tools
Python 3.6 Odoo 12+ XML HTML CSS SCSS PostgreSql Linux

Working in Notus in ERP team was really great, and there I had the best mentor in my carrier.

Working on ERP modules and project I was in touch with the biggest databases, over 1800 tables, which was really challenging to write optimized and great queries, especially for really important reports for accounting.

I am honored to work on the first module for salaries for Croatia, and here I learned about something that could help me in case I have my own company.

Unfortunately this team was the smallest, but was really efficient.
