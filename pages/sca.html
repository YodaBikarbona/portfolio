Smart Call Assistant

Smart Call Assistant is an innovative service designed to help people answer calls when they are unable to speak, using intelligent voice and text processing. The features include:

  • AI-powered call handling
  • Text-to-voice conversion for callers
  • Voice message responses
  • Flexible, customizable settings for different scenarios

The service is particularly helpful in situations like meetings, where you can't speak but still need to manage important calls. It acts as a personal assistant that intelligently responds on your behalf.

Stay productive and in control, even when your voice can't be heard!
